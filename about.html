We want to build products that make your life simple.

We believe that most “smart” devices actually aren’t. While they claim to make your life easier, they’re often complicated and require an electrician to install. We're here to change that. We started Switchmate to design the perfect light switch – one that is super simple to set up and that makes your everyday life just a little bit easier. We think we’ve done just that. Snap one on and see for yourself just how simple "smart" can be.
