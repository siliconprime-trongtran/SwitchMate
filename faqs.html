Questions?

Below are our most frequently asked questions. If your question isn't answered here, please don't hesitate to contact us!

What is Switchmate?

Switchmate empowers your switches with a snap. Switchmate lets you turn off your lights from your bed, keep your home actively lit when you're out, or have your lights turn on as you approach your home!

How do I use my Switchmate?

Use Switchmate with Bluetooth 4.0 enabled smartphones. Common smartphones with Bluetooth 4.0 include iPhone 4s and later (iOS), Samsung Galaxy S3/S4, and Nexus 4/5 (Android). With the optional hub, your Switchmates will work on any device with an internet connection!

How do I use my Switchmate hub?

The hub is not required to use Switchmate, but will allow you to control your Switchmates from any device with internet access!

How do I install my Switchmate?

Switchmate uses magnets to snap onto the screws of a standard light switch. Snap it on and connect to your smartphone! We will also provide adhesives in case you need them.

How will I know if Switchmate works on my switches?

If you have a standard toggle switch, a rocker/decora switch, or multi-gang versions of these switches, Switchmate will work for you!

What is my Switchmate's usable range?

Switchmate typically ranges up to 70 m (230 ft.) depending on where you are standing, as the signals could be absorbed by walls and doors. With the hub, you can use Switchmate from anywhere with internet access!

How long does my Switchmate battery last?

Switchmate will last for 8-12 months and is rechargeable.

Will the companion app be free?

Yes. Both our iPhone and Android apps will be free.
